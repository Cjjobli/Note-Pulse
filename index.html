Middle C is called middle C because it is in the middle of the grand staff.

The concept of Middle C has been used as a reference point in teaching and music theory because it sits in the middle of the practical vocal range of many singers and the range of many instruments. It helps in visualizing and understanding the structure of the musical scale and intervals.
